Frequently Asked Questions

What is Medicine Tracker?
Medicine Tracker is a web application that helps you manage and monitor your medicine stock, including their quantities and expiry dates.
How do I add a new medicine?
Simply use the "Add Medicine" form on the home page. Enter the name, quantity, and expiry date, then click submit.
What do the different box colors mean in the search results?
- Yellow: Medicine is available and not expired.
- Pink: Medicine is out of stock or both out of stock and expired.
-Green: Medicine is available and there is much time to expire.
- Red: Medicine is expired but still has some quantity.
Can I update or delete a medicine entry?
Yes. You can edit or delete any medicine entry directly from the list using the corresponding buttons.
Is my data saved permanently?
Currently, data is saved locally in your browser (localStorage). Clearing your browser data will remove the entries.
